04 / Features

Everything you need

Boarding Pass Scanner

Auto-detects barcodes from your camera. Supports all major airline formats including PDF417 and QR codes.

3D AR Arrows

Green holographic arrows projected onto the ground. Straight, turn left, turn right — they change with each step.

"Hey Aero" Voice AI

Always-listening wake word. Ask about bathrooms, coffee, WiFi, gates — Aero responds instantly with ElevenLabs voice.

Live Mini Map

Google Maps embedded in corner during navigation. Uses your GPS to show real-time position in the terminal.

Works Offline

PWA technology means the app works even without WiFi. Install it on any phone or computer for instant access.

Any Airport

Designed to work in any airport worldwide. From LAX to Dubai, Aero adapts to any terminal layout.

05 / How It Works

Three simple steps

01

Scan Your Boarding Pass

Open Aero and point your camera at your boarding pass. The barcode is detected automatically — your flight info, gate, seat, and boarding time appear instantly.

02

Follow the Arrows

Tap "Navigate" and 3D green arrows appear on the ground through your camera. They curve around corners, point through turns, and lead you step by step to your gate.

03

Ask Aero Anything

Say "Hey Aero, where's the nearest coffee shop?" and get an instant spoken response. The AI knows about bathrooms, restaurants, WiFi, gates, and more.
