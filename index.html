01 / The Glasses

Meet Aero glasses

Aero is a pair of lightweight AR smart glasses designed specifically for airport navigation. Slip them on when you arrive, and the entire terminal comes alive with directions.

A built-in camera scans your boarding pass automatically. A transparent display projects glowing green arrows onto the ground ahead of you. A microphone listens for your voice — just say "Hey Aero" and ask anything.

Transparent AR Display

See the real world with holographic overlays — arrows, signs, and info floating in your vision.

Built-in Microphone

Always-listening wake word: say "Hey Aero" and ask about bathrooms, food, WiFi, or your gate.

Barcode Camera

Auto-scans boarding passes — PDF417, QR, and all airline formats detected in under a second.

Step-by-Step Nav

3D green arrows on the ground — straight, turn left, turn right. Changes with every step you take.

03 / Features

Everything built in

3D AR Arrows

Green holographic arrows projected onto the ground. Straight, curved turns, arrival markers — they change with each step.

"Hey Aero" Voice AI

Always-listening wake word. Powered by Gemini AI and ElevenLabs voice. Ask anything — bathrooms, coffee, WiFi, gates.

Instant Pass Scanner

Point the glasses at any boarding pass barcode. Flight, gate, seat, boarding time — appears in your view instantly.

Live Mini Map

Google Maps embedded in the corner of your vision during navigation. Real-time GPS positioning in the terminal.

Any Airport

Works in all 17,000+ commercial airports worldwide. From LAX to Dubai to Tokyo — Aero adapts instantly.

Sound Alerts

Chime when navigation starts, success sound when you arrive. Turn-by-turn voice directions at every step.

Pick Up Your Glasses

Walk up to any Aero rental desk in the terminal. Grab a pair of pre-charged glasses. Put them on — no setup, no account, no app download.

⏱ Takes 30 seconds
02

Scan Your Boarding Pass

The built-in camera detects your boarding pass barcode automatically. Your flight, gate, seat, and boarding time appear right in your view.

📋 Auto-detected
03

Follow the Arrows

3D green holographic arrows appear on the ground ahead of you. They curve around corners, change direction at turns, and guide you step-by-step to your gate.

➔ Real-time AR
04

Ask Aero Anything

"Hey Aero, where's the nearest coffee shop?" — get instant spoken answers. Bathrooms, restaurants, WiFi, lounges — Aero knows it all.

🎤 Voice AI
05
